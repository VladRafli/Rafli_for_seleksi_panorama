How to make cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 110g softened butter
  • 200g golden caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g plain flour
  • 125 ml milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Cream together butter and sugar
  2. Beat eggs one at a time...
  3. And add vanilla
  4. Mix together backing powder and flour
  5. Beat into butter mixture in 2 batches
  6. Repeat with the milk
  7. Combine cocoa powder with water to form a paste
  8. Add the paste to half the mixture to make chocolate cupcakes
  9. Fill each case 3/4 full
  10. Bake 20 to 25 minutes in a 180 degrees celcius oven
  11. Let cool and top with your favorite icing
  12. Done! you accomplish making cupcakes
